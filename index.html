Ingredient

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (175C).
  2. Cream together the butter, sugar and brown sugar until smooth
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually mix into the creamed mixture
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips
  6. Drop dough by rounded spoonfuls unto ungreased baking sheets
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are golden brown
  8. Let cool on the wire racks
